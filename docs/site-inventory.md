## Process Content

### The 6-Step Empathy-Driven Brand Building™ Process

1. **Listen** - Initial discovery phase
2. **Learn** - Understand audience needs, challenges, values, goals, desires
3. **Create** - Develop brand persona and strategic messaging
4. **Communicate** - Share messaging using best-suited channels and tactics
5. **Engage** - Build real relationships through active listening and authentic interactions
6. **Evaluate** - Use data analytics and real-world feedback to refine and grow
